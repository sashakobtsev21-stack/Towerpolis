*Mid-2026. Grounded in current web data (sources at the bottom). Some figures are estimates/benchmarks, not guarantees.*

## 1. Market Snapshot

- **Online casual games market ≈ $22.68B (2026)**, ~5.41% CAGR → ~$29.5B by 2031.
- **Hybrid-casual is the growth engine:** IAP revenue **+20–37% YoY to ~$4.2B (2025)**, ~6B downloads. Broader casual: ~$32.7B IAP / 14.3B downloads. **Hyper-casual: ~22B downloads but tiny IAP — the dying model.**
- **Hybrid IAP grew ~100% YoY in Q2 2025; top-10 hybrid titles = $126M in one quarter; 36% of studios are mid-transition** hyper→hybrid in 2026.
- **Session frequency favors us:** puzzle/casual averages **~4.1 sessions/user/day** — the highest of any genre. Ideal for daily seeds and streaks.

### Proof points (simple core + meta = scale)

## 2. Competitor Landscape

| Competitor | What it is | Lesson for us |
|---|---|---|
| **Tower Bloxx / Tower Bloxx: My City** (Digital Chocolate) | The reference. Crane-drop stacker + "Build City" meta where towers populate a grid and raise population. | The city meta is **dormant and un-modernized** — no daily-social loop, no live-ops, frozen in ~2008. **This is our opening.** |
| **Stack** (Ketchapp) | Minimalist tap-stacker; canonical hyper-casual core + one-more-try replay. | Benchmark for the *pure core feel* and overhang-slice mechanic. |
| **Tower Crash 3D** | Modern 3D tap-to-drop stacker. | Shows the "tolerance shrinks as the tower grows" difficulty model. |
| Wobble/Tippy/Topple Tower clones | Saturated "wobble" space. | Names taken; differentiation must come from meta + daily seed, not the core stack. |

**The gap we exploit:** existing stackers are either (a) pure hyper-casual with no meta/retention, or (b) Tower Bloxx's city meta frozen in 2008. **No one offers a daily-seed competitive stacker + a persistent shareable 3D city.**

## 4. Naming

### Recommended: **Towerpolis** (Tower + metropolis)
- Keeps the #1 ASO root word **"Tower"**, one memorable coined word, premium feel.
- RU: transliterates cleanly to **Тауэрполис**; the "-полис" suffix already signals "city" (мегаполис, акрополь).
- **No live Google Play / App Store / Steam collision found.** Does **not** touch the protected "Bloxx" mark (Digital Chocolate/EA) or the Roblox "Blox" ecosystem, and avoids "Tower Defense" (Com2uS enforces).
- **Store-listing form:** `Towerpolis: Sky City` (EN) / `Towerpolis: Город в облаках` (RU) — loads ASO keywords (sky, city, tower, stack) without diluting the brand.
- ⚠️ **Diligence before launch:** run a formal **USPTO + EUIPO + Rospatent class 9/41** clearance search.

| Name | EN tagline | RU tagline | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Towerpolis** ✅ | *Stack the city to the sky.* | *Строй город до небес.* | Recommended. |
| **Cranetop** | *Drop. Stack. Rise.* | *Лови момент — строй ввысь.* | Backup 1. Names the literal crane mechanic; highly ownable. RU: Крейнтоп. |
| **Highstack** | — | — | Backup 2. "High" + "stack" (both ASO keywords); short/premium. RU: Хайстек. Verify exact one-word availability (the "stack" space is crowded). |
| Skyrise Stacker | — | — | Descriptive backup; clear and good as a subtitle. |
| Bloomtower / Heaventop | — | — | Coined, ownable; softer/cuter positioning. |

**Rejected (taken/risky):** Tower Bloxx/City Bloxx (protected IP), Stack/Stack Tower/Sky Stack/Stacko (live, generic, untrademarkable), Sky Tower/Skyrise-alone/Towerly/Skytopia (existing apps), Wobble/Tippy/Topple Tower (saturated), any "Blox-" name (Roblox confusion), anything with "Tower Defense" (enforced mark), Stackopolis (old Flash game — too close, hence the distinct "Towerpolis").
