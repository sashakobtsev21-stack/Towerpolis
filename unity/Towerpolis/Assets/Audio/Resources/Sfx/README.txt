Drop your SFX files here. Required exact names (extension can be .wav or .ogg):

  land      — plays every time a floor lands (Good or Perfect). SHORT & soft.
  perfect   — plays on a Perfect hit. Tonal (bell/chime/coin) — it pitches UP with combos.
  miss      — plays when a block misses.
  topple    — plays when the run ends (collapse).
  start     — plays at the start of a run (optional).

Example: Assets/Audio/Resources/Sfx/land.wav  ->  loaded as "Sfx/land".
Case matters in a build, so keep the names lowercase exactly as above.
No wiring needed — press Play and they're picked up automatically.

See docs/AUDIO_GUIDE.md for where to get free (CC0) sounds and recommended lengths.
